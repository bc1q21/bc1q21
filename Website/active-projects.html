Why bc1q21?

Your generosity supported by a system that facilitates smart-contract creation and timeless value.

1. Transparent

Benefit from the Bitcoin transactions' privacy with no further intervention from third parties - you keep full control over your money.

3. Reliable

The beneficiary is guaranteed the amount you decide on the dates you decide. The process allows the funds to be deployed with no missed payments and no hindrances.
